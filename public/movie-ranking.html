My top 3 movies of all time

Batman The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight has a gripping and well-written storyline that keeps viewers engaged from start to finish. The plot is filled with twists, turns, and moral dilemmas that add depth to the narrative.

The Prestige

The movie explores the theme of obsession, particularly in the pursuit of perfection and the rivalry between the two main characters, played by Hugh Jackman and Christian Bale. The psychological depth of the characters and their relentless pursuit of their craft adds a compelling dimension to the story.

Interstellar

The film features breathtaking visuals, including the depiction of distant planets, black holes, and space exploration. Director Christopher Nolan, along with cinematographer Hoyte van Hoytema, created visually striking scenes that contribute to the film's immersive experience.
